Pass/Fail Criteria

For P and PJ Type of Courses

1.)Students who pass the following criteria in the courses that they appeared are declared as "PASS".

  • For P Type of Courses
  • TEE Marks(Lab assessment)>=50%

    • And

  • Continuous Lab Assessment+TEE Marks>=50 %
  • For PJ Type of Courses

Review 1(out of 20)+Review 2(out of 30)+ Review 3(out of 50)>=50
