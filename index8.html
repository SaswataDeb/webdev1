Pass/Fail Criteria for LT,LTP,LP Type Of Courses.

Students who satisfy the following criteria in the courses that they appeared are declared as "PASS:.

  • TEE Marks(out of 50 in online) >=20

    • AND

  • CAT-1(out of 50)+CAT-2(out of 50)+TEE(out of 100)(offline)>=80(out of 200)
    • OR
    Mid-Term(out of 50)+TEE(out of 50 in online)>=40(out of 100)
