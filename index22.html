The Cumulative Grade Point Average(CGPA)

  • CGPA indicates the overall performance of a student from the time he/she joined the university to a specific semester.
  • It is obtained by calculating the weighted average of the grade points obtained in all the courses registered by the student since the first semester.
CGPA= Summation from i=1 to n (Course credit*Grade point)/Summation from i=1 to n (Course credit)
