Calculation of Grand Total Marks for LT,LTP,LP type of courses.

Assessment and Evaluation Mark Components for LTP,LT and LP Types of courses

Grand Total= CAT1 marks*0.3+CAT2 marks*0.3+TEE marks*0.3+Attendance marks+Other Assessment Marks.

OR
Grand Total=Mid-Term marks*0.6+TEE marks*0.3+Attendance marks+Other Assessment Marks.

EXAMPLE

Mid-Term(50) Attendance % Total Assessment Marks(35) TEE Marks(100)
34 88.2% 32.5 77
Grand Total=Mid-Term marks*0.6+TEE marks*0.3+Attendance marks+Other Assessment Marks.
=34+77*0.3+3+32.5
=20.4+23.1+3+32.5
=79
