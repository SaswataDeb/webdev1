Grade Improvement

  • If a student opts for grade improvement,then the course has to be re-registered.Re-registration fee will be as per the university norms existing at the time of registration.
  • When a course is re-registered,all earlier course evaluation marks shall be treated as cancelled/reset.
  • Course improvement can be availed only once for a given course and only one course can be registered for course improvement per semester.
  • Students at THEIR GRADUATING YEAR or timed out students are permitted to register more than one 'grade improvement' courses, in order to improve their CGPA.
