Calculation of Grades

Standard Deviation=SD
Relative Grading Formula Letter Grade
Total Marks>=(Mean+1.5*SD) S
Total Marks>=(Mean+0.5*SD) And Total Marks less than(Mean+1.5*SD) A
Total Marks>=(Mean-0.5*SD) And Total Marks less than(Mean+0.5*SD) B
Total Marks>=(Mean-1.0*SD) And Total Marks less than(Mean-0.5*SD) C
Total Marks>=(Mean-1.5*SD) And Total Marks less than(Mean-1.0*SD) D
TEE>=40% And (CAT1+CAT2+TEEOR Mid-Term+TEE>=40%) And (Total Marks less than Mean-1.5*SD) E
TEE less than 40 percent OR(CAT1+CAT2+TEE less than 40 percent OR Mid-Term+TEE less than 40 percent) F
